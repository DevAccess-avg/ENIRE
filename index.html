Cybersecurity Intervention Directorate • Incident Reference: CID‑2026‑07‑17‑A91

Access to this domain has been terminated

This online service has been disabled following the discovery of automated data‑harvesting behavior capable of extracting sensitive user information without consent.
Summary of detected behavior
  • Credential Harvesting: Attempted extraction of saved passwords from local browsers and credential vaults.
  • Token Collection: Automated retrieval of session tokens from installed applications.
  • Identity Scraping: Attempts to read system‑stored personal identifiers including legal name, billing profiles, and autofill data.
  • Device Enumeration: Scanning of installed apps, linked accounts, and active authentication sessions.
  • Social Platform Mapping: The service attempted to enumerate linked social accounts. Confirmed targets included:
    • Discord
    • Snapchat
    • TikTok
    • YouTube
    • Twitch
    • Instagram
    • Twitter/X
    • Steam
    • Reddit
  • Network Profiling: Collection of IP history, Wi‑Fi SSID metadata, and device fingerprinting signatures.
  • System Telemetry: Attempts to access clipboard history, running processes, and hardware identifiers.
Reason for service termination
  • Violation: Unauthorized collection of user credentials and personal data.
  • Risk Level: High — activity consistent with credential‑theft automation and cross‑platform account takeover attempts.
  • Action: Immediate shutdown of public access and isolation of associated infrastructure.
